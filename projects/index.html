Purpose

Registry, queue, and routing

Projects collects deliberate exports and manual records, classifies them correctly, and keeps downstream portals pulling from a shared production registry instead of isolated page-local state.

Owns
  • project records
  • class + target portal
  • status, routing, and notes
  • links to chat, pipelines, and portal work
  • production-ready registry state
Does not own
  • open-ended chat discussion
  • deep pipeline graph editing
  • deep research corpus ownership
  • deep lore/game design archive ownership
