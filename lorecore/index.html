Backend truth
  • GET /api/lorecore/overview loads top-level LoreCore state.
  • POST /api/lorecore/sessions/{session_public_id}/messages sends discussion messages.
  • POST /api/lorecore/exports exports current LoreCore context.
  • POST /api/lorecore/books and PUT /api/lorecore/books/{book_public_id} are real.
  • POST /api/lorecore/books/{book_public_id}/run-stage is real.
  • GET /api/lorecore/drafts and GET /api/lorecore/notes are real.
