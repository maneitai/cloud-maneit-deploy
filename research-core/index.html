Purpose

Research substrate for the whole platform

Research Core is where questions become source stacks, extracts, syntheses, and reusable evidence chains that can strengthen portal creation, software work, pipelines, lore, and game design.

Owns
  • source library and corpus
  • research chats and notes
  • dossiers and evidence chains
  • syntheses, eval packs, and training sets
Does not own
  • final app or portal implementation
  • final lore archive ownership
  • final game-system ownership
